# Ilumionate Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: March 5, 2026
**Last Updated**: March 5, 2026

## Overview

Ilumionate ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Ilumionate app.

## Information We Collect

### Data Processed Locally
All core app functionality occurs entirely on your device. We do not transmit personal data to external servers.

**Audio Files**:
- Audio files you import remain on your device only
- Audio analysis happens entirely on-device using Apple's Core ML
- No audio content is uploaded, transmitted, or stored externally

**Light Session Data**:
- Session preferences and custom configurations stored locally
- Playlist data and session history stored in app's local storage
- No session data transmitted to external servers

**App Usage Data** (Optional):
- Anonymous app performance metrics (if you opt-in)
- Crash reports to help improve stability (if you opt-in)
- No personal identifiers included in any analytics

### Data Not Collected
We do NOT collect:
- Personal identification information
- Audio file contents
- Session content or details
- Location data
- Contacts or calendar information
- Photos or camera access

## Permissions Required

### Microphone Access (If Recording)
**Purpose**: Only used if you choose to record audio directly in the app
**Usage**: Audio recording happens locally on your device
**Storage**: Recorded audio stored only on your device
**Note**: You can use the app fully without microphone access by importing existing audio files

### Audio Playback
**Purpose**: Playing audio during light therapy sessions
**Usage**: Standard audio playback through your device speakers/headphones
**Storage**: No audio data collected or transmitted

### File System Access
**Purpose**: Importing audio files from your device
**Usage**: Reading audio files you select from your device storage
**Storage**: Files remain in their original location and in app's local storage

## Data Sharing

**We do not share, sell, or transmit any personal data to third parties.**

All data processing occurs on your device using:
- Apple's on-device machine learning frameworks
- Local file storage
- Local audio processing

## Data Security

### On-Device Processing
- All AI analysis uses Apple's Core ML running locally
- No data leaves your device during analysis
- Audio files encrypted using iOS standard encryption

### Local Storage
- All app data stored using iOS secure storage mechanisms
- Data protected by your device's passcode and encryption
- No cloud storage or external database connections

## Children's Privacy

Ilumionate is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

## Your Rights

### Data Control
- All your data remains under your control on your device
- Delete data by removing the app or clearing app storage
- No account creation required - no external data to manage

### Opt-Out Options
- Anonymous analytics can be disabled in Settings
- All permissions can be revoked in iOS Settings
- App functions fully without optional permissions

## Medical Disclaimer

- Discontinue use if you experience discomfort or adverse effects
